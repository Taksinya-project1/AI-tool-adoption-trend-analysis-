# AI Company Adoption Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes AI adoption across companies using Python, Google Colab, and Power BI. The analysis explores AI adoption trends, productivity improvements, revenue growth, employee satisfaction, and business performance through statistical analysis and interactive data visualization.

## Objectives

* Analyze AI adoption across different industries.
* Study the impact of AI on productivity and revenue.
* Compare AI adoption among different company sizes.
* Perform hypothesis testing using statistical methods.
* Create an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## Key Findings

* AI adoption positively impacts productivity.
* Higher AI adoption is associated with improved revenue growth.
* Employee satisfaction remains stable with effective AI implementation.
* AI investment supports long-term business performance.
* Significant productivity differences exist between Startup and Enterprise companies.
